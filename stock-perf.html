Stock Non-Special BoP Car PP Listing

Last updated for v1.36

This data is also available in JSON and CSV formats so you can import it as a spreadsheet / to other apps.
Only non-Group (1/2/3/4/B) cars are displayed as they use 100%/100% power/weight for BoP (equal to this stock performance).
The "Gr.N" and "Gr.X" displayed match their use in GT Sport: however, they are not displayed to the user in GT7.
"Gr.X" is highlighted as these are usually racing or otherwise special cars that you may wish to exclude from your own online events.
This list is provided for quickly finding BoP road cars at a specific PP value on a specific tyre, which you may find useful for some custom lobbies.

The list is sorted by performance on SS (Sports Soft) tyres, as the kart has no other tyre & the 500F / 595 SS fail the PP test on Racing tyres.
