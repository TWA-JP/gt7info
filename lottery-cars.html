Ticket Reward Cars

Last updated for v1.27

Obtainable from 1/2/3/4 star tickets:

    %LOTTERY_CAR_S

Obtainable from 3/4/5 star tickets:

    %LOTTERY_CAR_M

Obtainable from 4/5/6 star tickets:

    %LOTTERY_CAR_L

Obtainable as a special gift on your birthday:

    %LOTTERY_CAR_B

Note: special parts can currently only be obtained for cars in one of these categories.
This may change in the future.
